Hurricane Lee Revs Up

As Hurricane Lee builds momentum in the Atlantic Ocean, it approaches the east coast as a category 3 hurricane. It is recommended to secure belongings and stay inside to prepare for the approaching storm. Although the storm is sitll churning in the Atlantic Ocean, some reports say that it could make Landfall in North Carolina as a category 3 or 4 storm.

Some projections have the eye of the storm right here, in Raleigh, North Carolina on September 19th. One thing that we can hope for, however, is that classes will be cancelled and we can have an extra fall break.

Ben Shelton moves onto Semi-final of US Open

After a 3-1 Set victory over Frances Tiafoe on Tuesday night, 20-year old Ben Shelton has advanced to the semi finals of the Men's US Open. He will face Novak Djokovic on Friday, who is currently ranked Number 2 in the world.
